24 Fifth Avenue, #429
A grand building with Premier Location, at the corner of 9th street and Fifth Avenue in the Heart of Greenwich Village. Originally it was built in 1926 by Emery Roth as the Fifth Avenue Hotel.    The building offers 24hr concierge, laundry room, gym,and bike storage. The spectacular lobby has recently been renovated to the original splendor taking one back to it's magnificent past.     This is an oversized prewar one bedroom with a gracious entry way leading to a substantial living room with four deep closets.
